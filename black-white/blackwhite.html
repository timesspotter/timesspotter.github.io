What colour is solitude?
Try some: green, red, blue.
If you don't like - try yellow,
The sunniest colour of all.
Try each one,
and solitude will run.
Away for a while. To return back
When you don't expect.

To portray the traits of the nation, to exclude the husk of its history I tried to restrict colorfulness of some pictures. Simple and straight Black and clear and vibrant White, what could be better for the task. Only some impregnation of other less important colors.
